  • General Admission Conditions

    In Turkey, central placement and special talent exams are applied in the admission of students to associate and undergraduate programs of Higher Education Institutions. central placement; It is done with the Higher Education Institutions Exam (YKS) conducted by the Measurement, Selection and Placement Center (ÖSYM). The first stage of the exam, which consists of two stages, is the Basic Proficiency Test (TYT), and the second stage is the Field Proficiency Test (AYT). Candidates who exceed 180 points can participate in AYT. AYT; It consists of Turkish, Basic Mathematics, Social Sciences and Science tests. Candidates who get 180 points out of 500 points in AYT can choose undergraduate programs. Candidates are placed in undergraduate programs via computer by ÖSYM, based on the scores they get from TYT and AYT and their secondary education (high school in the Turkish Education System) achievement scores.

  • Academic Counseling

    Associate and Undergraduate Level

    A faculty member is appointed to each of the students enrolled in Eskişehir Technical University to provide consultancy in every subject within the framework of the "Eskişehir Technical University Academic Counseling Directive". From the beginning to the end of the student period, the advisor lecturer provides consultancy services to the students in every subject they need, especially in academic subjects; It helps especially in course selection and registration renewal processes.


    Master's Level

    Upon the recommendation of the Graduate School Department and the decision of the Graduate School Administrative Board, by taking the opinions of the student in the master's program with thesis and the faculty member who is recommended to undertake the consultancy, a faculty member is appointed until the end of the first semester to advise each student in the course selection and the execution of the master's thesis. In the non-thesis master's program, upon the recommendation of the Institute Department, a faculty member or doctoral lecturer is appointed by the Institute Administrative Board to advise each student in the course selection and execution of the "Semester Project" until the end of the first semester at the latest.
