Library Facilities

With its strong electronic resource collection, 130 000+ e-books and 30 000+ printed book collection, students and academics are physically served in our central library and reading room. Remote access to the e-resources in the library is also provided.

Student Clubs

Student clubs of our university are waiting for you in order to develop individual talents, gain the ability to work in a team, and meet the social and cultural needs of our students. Our university supports our students to join the clubs of their choice or to establish their own club.

Sports Facilities and Campus Recreation

Our university hosts panels, congresses and symposiums in sports halls, stadiums and qualified conference halls capable of organizing international Olympics and competitions, and offers students a unique campus atmosphere in both scientific and social terms.
